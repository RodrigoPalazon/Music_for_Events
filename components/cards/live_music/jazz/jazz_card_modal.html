Carolina Peña

Carolina Peña, best known as Caro, is a young professional jazz singer based in The Hague, Netherlands. Born in Quito, Ecuador, she pursued music from a young age.

She is a young talent which spread her culture through boleros and bossa-nova music with the influence of jazz. She is a very active musician in The Hague, performing almost every week.

In October 2022, she was invited to open Hans Dulfer concert. Plus, she has been invited to share her musical talent in multiple international stages, in The United States, South America and around The Netherlands.

Currently, Caro is continuing her musical career in the Koninklijk Conervatorium Den Haag.
