ABOUT US

The text over the picture that says: About ‘Music for events’ – Change to: Get To Know Us.

Musicforevents.nl is an event company founded in the Netherlands by a group of professional musicians and artists who bring the highest quality of service to their customers. Our services include live music, DJs, photographers and videographers.

We collaborate only with the most talented and passionate professionals to make sure that every event is a success. Our team is at your disposal to make planning easier, quicker, and bring your event to the next level. Through the versatility of our carefully selected entertainers, you will find the perfect match for your event. Options include classical music, Jazz bands, 1920’s music, singers, party bands, all around DJs, and much more. Navigate our website to find your ideal band or send us a message and tell us about you and your event. We will help you every step of the way ensuring that your event will be one for the memories.

Do you have a special request, a special song or a fantastic idea for the perfect photo? We would love to hear about it and make it happen! Our company provides a personalized service, turning your dreams into a reality. Send us an email, call us, or schedule an appointment for a video call or an in person meeting. We look forward to providing you our services so that you'll be able to relive your special day over and over again. Book us for your event and create a day you will not forget.
